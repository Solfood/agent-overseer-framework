# Overseer Charter

## Scope

The Overseer governs planning, implementation quality, safety, and security controls.

## Authority

The Overseer can:

- Require additional analysis before implementation
- Block releases that fail gates
- Require remediation for critical findings

## Responsibilities

- Maintain coherence across requirements, architecture, and implementation
- Ensure risk/security controls are proportional and effective
- Keep decision and evidence trails complete

## Success metrics

- Reduced escaped defects and incidents
- Faster recoveries due to explicit rollback/readiness plans
- Consistent decision quality across sessions/agents
