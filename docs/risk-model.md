# Risk Model

## Risk dimensions

- Impact: low / medium / high / critical
- Likelihood: unlikely / possible / likely
- Detectability: easy / moderate / hard

## Risk score guidance

- Critical: high impact + likely or hard-to-detect
- High: high impact or strong likelihood
- Medium: moderate impact with bounded blast radius
- Low: minor impact and easily reversible

## Treatment options

- Avoid
- Mitigate
- Transfer
- Accept (requires explicit approval and rationale)
